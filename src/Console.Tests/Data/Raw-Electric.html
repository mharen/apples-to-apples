Apples to Apples Comparison Chart 

Ohio Edison 

To best utilize this offer comparison tool, it is suggested that you have your most current utility bill available for reference. Compare the supplier offers contained in the chart with the "Price to Compare" shown on your electric bill.

 

Ohio Edison Company's residential “Price to Compare” for the generation supply portion of your bill for the period January 1, 2026 through March 31, 2026 is $0.0933 per kWh*. 

 
 
*If you are a qualifying electric heating customer your “Price to Compare” may vary from the “Price to Compare” above during the months of Sept 1-May 31. Please locate your actual “Price to Compare” during these months within the message section of your bill.
Sign up for Ohio Edison rate change alerts
 
The offer prices below reflect that of the generation portion of your bill. Your distribution and transmission rates are determined through your local utility company.
